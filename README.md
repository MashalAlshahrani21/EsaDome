# 🧠 ESA-DOME

> **Building Responsible AI Learning for the Age of Artificial Intelligence**

Helping students **learn with AI instead of letting AI learn for them.**

**Founder & Project Lead:** Mashal Alshahrani
---

## 🌍 About ESA-DOME

ESA-DOME is an educational technology platform designed to help students, teachers, schools, and parents navigate the growing role of Artificial Intelligence in education.

Instead of simply detecting AI-generated work, ESA-DOME focuses on understanding **how AI is being used**, encouraging responsible learning habits, promoting academic integrity, and helping students remain independent thinkers.

Our goal is not to ban AI.

Our goal is to teach students how to use AI responsibly.

---

# 🚀 The Problem

Artificial Intelligence has fundamentally changed education.

Students can now use AI to:

- ✍️ Write essays
- 📚 Summarize textbooks
- 💻 Generate code
- ➗ Solve mathematics
- 🧠 Answer homework instantly

AI is incredibly powerful—but it also introduces new challenges.

Current AI detection tools often:

- ❌ Produce false positives
- ❌ Punish instead of educate
- ❌ Ignore learning context
- ❌ Cannot distinguish responsible AI use from academic dishonesty
- ❌ Provide little or no educational feedback

Schools need better tools that encourage learning—not fear.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

ESA-DOME is an AI transparency and education platform.

Instead of asking:

> **"Did the student use AI?"**

We ask:

> **"How was AI used?"**

By focusing on behavior instead of punishment, ESA-DOME helps schools encourage responsible AI usage while giving students the opportunity to learn and improve.

---

# 🏛️ The DOME Framework

**DOME = Digital Oversight & Mindful Engagement**

The DOME Framework is the educational foundation behind ESA-DOME.

Rather than automatically punishing students, DOME helps them recognize three major risks of AI misuse:

- 📄 Plagiarism
- 🧠 Cognitive Atrophy
- ⚠️ AI Overreliance

Students receive educational feedback and are encouraged to reflect on their AI habits before unhealthy behaviors become normal.

---

# 🔍 ESA-DOME Scan

The heart of the platform.

ESA-DOME Scan analyzes learning behavior rather than simply detecting AI.

It can identify:

- AI usage patterns
- Copy-and-paste behavior
- Learning interactions
- Potential shortcut risks
- Responsible AI habits

The goal is awareness—not punishment.

---

# 💬 AI Reflection System

When potentially risky AI behavior is detected, students are asked to explain how AI was used.

Example questions include:

- Did AI help you learn?
- Did you brainstorm ideas?
- Did AI revise your writing?
- Did AI complete the assignment for you?

Students have the opportunity to provide context before any conclusions are made.

---

# 📈 Weekly AI Score

Students receive a weekly learning report showing how AI was used.

Example:

AI Usage This Week

Total Sessions: 12

✅ Learning-Based: 7

✍️ Writing Assistance: 3

⚠️ Shortcut Risk: 2

Instead of only identifying problems, ESA-DOME helps students improve their learning habits over time.

---

# 📚 Use AI Better

ESA-DOME teaches students how to make AI a learning tool instead of a shortcut.

### Good AI Habits

- ✅ Ask AI to explain concepts
- ✅ Request study guides
- ✅ Brainstorm ideas
- ✅ Compare multiple solutions
- ✅ Learn step-by-step

### Avoid

- ❌ Copying AI answers
- ❌ Submitting AI-generated work
- ❌ Replacing critical thinking
- ❌ Becoming dependent on AI

# 🔒 Browser Extension Limitations

Modern browsers intentionally prevent extensions from controlling an entire device.

ESA-DOME **does not** attempt to:

- Lock student computers
- Prevent extension removal
- Block operating system applications
- Force device-wide monitoring

Instead, ESA-DOME focuses on platform integrity.

The extension can:

- Send heartbeat signals
- Verify active sessions
- Detect disconnections
- Require re-authentication
- Notify instructors when sessions become untrusted

This approach balances security with user privacy.

# 🔐 Privacy & Ethics

Privacy is one of ESA-DOME's core principles.

We believe AI transparency should never come at the cost of student trust.

Our principles include:

- Educational transparency
- Human review before disciplinary action
- Minimal data collection
- Ethical AI use
- Explainable AI indicators
- Student privacy by design

ESA-DOME exists to promote responsible AI literacy—not surveillance.
